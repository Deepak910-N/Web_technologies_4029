Olympics

The Olympics is a major international multi-sport event featuring summer and winter sports competitions. Athletes from around the world compete for medals in various disciplines, showcasing their skills and representing their countries. The Games are held every four years, with the Summer and Winter Olympics alternating every two years. The modern Olympic Games began in 1896, inspired by the ancient Greek Olympics. They are known for their celebration of global unity, sportsmanship, and excellence.
